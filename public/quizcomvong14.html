    Opportunity costs

    Who should specialized in producing which products?

    Answer: Point B

    Explanation:

    Mrs. Phuong and Me Tri village have to forgo 1/4 and 1/2 cốm sticky rice, respectively, to produce a cốm patty, so Mrs. Phuong should specialized in producing cốm patty. Similarly, Mrs. Phuong and Me Tri village have to give up 4 and 2 cốm patties respectively to make a cốm sticky rice, so Me Tri village should specialized in producing sticky rice.
