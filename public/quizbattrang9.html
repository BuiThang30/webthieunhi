    Let's assume, for some reason, that pottery is the most important industry in Vietnam's economy. There has been a recent TikTok trend that greatly increased the popularity of pottery among youths, so millions of people are flocking to buy these ornaments. What is a possible impact on the rate of inflation in Vietnam? What type of inflation is this?

    Answer: Point A

    Explanation:

    If you can produce more pottery with the same resources (e.g., time, labor, materials), you have an absolute advantage over other artisans because you're more efficient in production. However, do not that absolute advantage is simply a subset of comparative advantage. Hence, it is not wrong as well to say that you have comparative advantage in this case. Just for the sake of differentiation, we shall make that distinction here.
