    Bat Trang Pottery Village, located in the outskirts of Hanoi, Vietnam, is a renowned traditional craft village with a rich history of over 700 years. Known for its high-quality ceramics, Bat Trang has become a cultural hub where artisans create intricate pottery using techniques passed down through generations. The village attracts both local and international tourists who come to explore its workshops, learn about the pottery-making process, and purchase beautifully handcrafted ceramics, ranging from vases and dishes to artistic sculptures. Bat Trang stands as a symbol of Vietnam's enduring craftsmanship and artistic heritage.
