    Demand

    These customers come to your store:

    • Customer A is craving for a bowl of pho but does not bring any money to buy.

    • Customer B brings some money, but upon looking at the menu, he does not want to have a bowl.

    • Customer C goes right in, orders a bowl of pho and pays you.

    Which customers construct the demand for Little John’s store?

    Answer: Point C

    Explanation:

    The demand for a good is constructed when consumers both can and want to buy the good on offer. Customer A wants pho but cannot buy it without any money. Customer B has the money, but he doesn’t want to eat pho. Only customer C has the money to buy pho and indeed wants a bowl of pho.
