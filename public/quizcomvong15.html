    Opportunity costs

    You now have to offer a plausible price for each Me Tri village sticky rice

    Answer: Point B

    Explanation:

    Ordinarily, Me Tri village can produce 1 sticky rice by giving up 2 cốm patties, so they would only consent to trade 1 sticky rice for more than 2 cốm patties. Meanwhile, Mrs. Phuong can produce a cốm sticky rice by forgoing 4 cốm patties, so she would only buy a cốm sticky rice with a price of less than 4 cốm patties. Therefore, 3 cốm patties for 1 cốm sticky rice is a reasonable deal
