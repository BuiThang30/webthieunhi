    Introduction to Supply:

    Unlike industrial ceramics, which benefit from automated production processes, your handcrafted pottery involves more labor-intensive and time-consuming methods. What is a potential challenge to the supply of handcrafted pottery compared to industrial ceramics?

    Answer: Point B

    Explanation:

    Being more labor-intensive and time-consuming means your shop requires more labor - which incurs higher wages - and more time to produce the same amount of products as the industrial ceramic producers. Thus, your average costs of production are much higher than them.
