    The farmlands(the main supplier of cốm) throughout Vietnam has been affected aggressively by a recent storm. Now, you need to predict how will your competitors react to this event so that you can prepare your countermeasure

    Answer: Point B

    Explanation:

    This is a possible but not reasonable choice for them since bánh gai is also a food that are mostly made of crop-related ingredients.

    The production costs will likely increase due to that storm, which forces the price to rise. By predicting reasonably, this a good fundamental for your strategy

    This is also a possible scenario(hard to happen) if they had stored all the cốm somewhere before the storm came(this is the case where they did a good prediction). However, it's not optimal for them to apply promotions to products' price.

    Keeping the price unchanged will cause them losses in revenue since the quantity supplied must be reduced due to the decrease in ingredients. Hence, they have no incentive to do so
