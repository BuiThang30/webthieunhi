    Market and Types of Market:

    Your pottery shop operates in a competitive environment where many artisans produce unique pottery items. What type of market structure is the Bat Trang pottery industry most similar to?

    Answer: Point C

    Explanation:

    Bat Trang pottery involves many artisans producing differentiated pottery items. While each seller offers different designs, they still compete with others, and there are many producers still - making it a monopolistic competition market.
