    Elasticity of demand

    There are actually many cốm stores in Hanoi, but you are quite sure that there are not that many places where they could make cốm as good as here. Given the fact that Hanoians are gourmets, what would probably be the elasticity of demand for cốm of Mrs. Phuong’s store?

    Answer: Point C

    Explanation:

    - Hanoians are picky eaters, they always want the most delicious food, regardless of its price, so as long as Mrs. Phuong can make good cốm, the demand would not be elastic. Moreover, there are few substitutes for equal-quality products.

    Mrs. Phuong could make proper cốm, but there are still some substitutes, if the price is too high, they would look for other suppliers. As long as the price is reasonable, the demand for cốm would not alter much. Hence, the demand is quite inelastic, which means customers do not react considerably when the price is changed.

    It can’t be very inelastic because there are substitutes.
