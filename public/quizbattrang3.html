    Introduction to Demand:

    Your handcraft pottery sales have dropped by 30% due to the influx of cheaper industrial ceramics. You need to boost your revenue quickly. You need a strategy to regain market share soon while maintaining your brand’s integrity.

    Answer: Point C

    Explanation:

    According to the law of demand, lowering price does increase the quantity demanded. However, do note that ceteris paribus does not hold in reality, so your competitors are likely to decrease price as well, making everyone worse. Instead, a marketing campaign which shifts the consumers’ taste and preferences in favor of your productions will increase the demand for your pottery and cause your market share to rise.
