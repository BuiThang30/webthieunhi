And guess what, after banh mi, pho is the second word added to the Oxford dictionary, inducing a huge surge in preferences, thus demand, for this delicacy. People are flocking to have for themselves bowls of pho every morning. And what kind of Hanoian pho does not come with youtiao (a kind of fried dough usually enjoyed with Vietnamese noodles)?

    It is a MUST to buy youtiao when having pho.

    How will this increase in demand for pho affect the demand for youtiao?

    Answer: Point D

    Explanation:

    Youtiao and pho in this case have joint demand - that is, they are goods that are often used together. When a customer enjoys a bowl of pho, that customer tends to order a bowl of youtiao to complement it as well. Therefore, if the demand for pho increases, the demand for youtiao will also increase!
