    Types of market

    First and foremost, you need to decide which type of market Mrs. Phuong is participating in. After some time of research, you figure out that there are many cốm stores in Hanoi, but what they sell are not the same, some are xôi cốm, some are chả cốm, chè cốm,... However, the main suppliers of cốm are only 2 villages: Vòng village - where you are in, and Mễ trì village.

    Answer: Point A

    Explanation:

    There are many stores, but they are not similar - features of a monopolistic competition market. This knowledge will help you build your business strategy in the future

    What they sell are different, so it can’t be the Perfect competition market.

    There are a lot of stores, so that’s not Monopoly

    This could have been the case if you decided to produce Cốm only. However, we also produce different kinds of food made of cốm. Hence, this is not an Oligopolistic market. This wrong classification could result in a false step in the future
