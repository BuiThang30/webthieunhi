    Mrs. Phương plan to trade with Mễ Trì village to boost our revenues. The table below shows the time each store need to make a cốm patty or a cốm sticky rice. Decide who has the absolute advantage in each product.

    Com patty Sticky rice
    Me Tri Village 3 hours 6 hours
    Mrs. Phuong 2 hours 8 hours

    Answer: Point C

    Explanation:

    Me Tri village need less time to produce cốm sticky rice, so they have absolute advantage in producing that type of food. Likewwise, Mrs. Phuong can produce cốm patty faster, so she has absolute advantage in producing it
